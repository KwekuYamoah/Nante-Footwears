Home FAQs
Do You Do Nationwide Deliveries?

Yes, Nante does nationwide deliveries. Deliveries have been outsourced to the State Transport Company(STC). Our partnership with them ensures a smooth delivery process for you our lovely customers

Can I Return A Footwear If It Does Fit My Feet?

The current policy for the returning of ordered goods is 7 days after delivery. It is our hope that customers maintain the quality of the footwear so that they re-purchase another one or ask for a refund.

Can I Contact Customer Care?

Yes you can contact customer care. Nante exists for the sake of its customers. Customer Care be contacted on the Contact Page. You fill the form and a customer care rep will get back to you within 2-3 working days
